## GENERAL TIPS

### Subject Line Best Practices
- Keep under 50 characters
- Personalize when possible
- Ask a question or offer value
- Avoid: FREE, URGENT, all caps

### Send Times
- Best: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 4-6 PM
- Avoid: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, weekends

### Follow-Up Cadence
- Day 0: Initial send
- Day 3: First follow-up
- Day 7: Second follow-up
- Day 14: Third follow-up
- Day 30: Re-engagement check
- Day 90: Cold re-engagement

### Metrics to Track
- Open rate (target: 30%+)
- Reply rate (target: 5%+)
- Meeting booked rate (target: 20%+ of replies)
