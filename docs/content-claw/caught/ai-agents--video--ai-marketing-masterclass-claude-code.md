## Key Framework: Research-First AI Marketing

**Core Philosophy:** Most AI marketing fails because people jump straight into prompting without context. The magic happens in the upfront research and skill-building phase.

### The Workflow:

1. **Research Phase (1 hour)** - Use Perplexity MCP for deep market research
   - Analyze competitors
   - Find market gaps  
   - Build rich context documents
   - Load context before any asset creation

2. **Skills Development** - Create instruction manuals for AI agents
   - The expert perspective you build in (last 10-20%) separates great output from "AI slop"
   - Skills act as specialized agents for different marketing functions

3. **End-to-End Execution** - Complete marketing system in Claude Code:
   - Landing page
   - Lead magnet  
   - Ad creative
   - SEO content
   - Traffic strategy
   - All built and deployed from single environment
