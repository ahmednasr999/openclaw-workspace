PMO + Governance
4 Layers.
15 Hospitals.
One Framework.
Multi-site programs fail when you centralize the wrong thing. Here's what actually holds it together.
1
The Unchangeables (Program Core)
Scope boundaries. Budget governance. Executive steering cadence. If it touches patient safety or regulatory risk, it's locked.
2
The Adapters (Country-Level)
Regulatory alignment to local MOH requirements. Cultural protocols. KSA, UAE, and Egypt each get their own playbook.
3
The Customizers (Site-Level)
Each hospital gets a tailored sequence based on current maturity. Not a standardized roadmap. The site owns execution.
4
The Integrators (Program Intelligence)
Weekly signal aggregation from all 15 sites. Pattern recognition. Proactive escalation before issues become crises.
Centralize the governance. Decentralize the execution.
The program office sets the rules. The sites own the game.
