Standardize Outputs. Never the Humans.

2

The PMO is a Translation Function

Not reporting. Not admin. Translating business intent into local execution and local friction into executive signal.

3

Frameworks Come From Scar Tissue

The best PMO frameworks aren't built from certifications. They're built from getting it wrong in 8 different time zones and learning fast.
