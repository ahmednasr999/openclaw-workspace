## Matching Rules

A post qualifies for engagement if:
1. It matches keywords from at least 2 categories above
2. It was published in the last 18 hours
3. It has at least 10 reactions (signals visibility, not dead content)
4. The author is either on the targets list OR has 5,000+ followers

## Comment Drafting Guidelines

- Lead with a contrarian or additive insight (not agreement)
- Reference a specific number or experience when possible
- Keep to 2-3 sentences max
- Never mention job searching or "looking for opportunities"
- End with a question that invites further discussion (boosts comment visibility)
