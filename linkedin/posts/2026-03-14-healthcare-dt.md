## Post

**Something to think about:**

In 2025, I led a $50M digital transformation across 15 hospitals in Saudi Arabia.

The hardest part wasn't the technology.

It was changing how 5,000 people thought about their jobs.

Healthcare isn't broken. It just needs to think differently about what "done" looks like.

If you're in the middle of a transformation, the question isn't "are we moving fast enough?"

It's "are we moving in the right direction?"

What's your biggest DT challenge right now?
