**Stakeholder Mapping Template: Multi-Site Transformation**

**Column 1: Stakeholder Name and Role**
Simple. Don't skip this. Every name matters.

**Column 2: Site / Country**
Because the same role title behaves differently in different geographies.

**Column 3: Influence Level (1-5)**
Not their seniority. Their actual influence over adoption.
The head nurse on ward 3 might be a 5. The deputy CIO might be a 2.

**Column 4: Interest Level (1-5)**
How much do they care about this program? High interest + high influence = your champions.
High influence + low interest = your biggest risk.

**Column 5: Current Stance**
Supporter / Neutral / Resistant. Be honest. Don't write what you wish was true.

**Column 6: What They Need**
Not what you want to tell them. What they need to move.
Data? Reassurance? A win they can claim? A seat at the table?

**Column 7: Engagement Frequency**
Weekly. Monthly. Milestone-based. Match frequency to influence level.

**Column 8: Owner**
Who on your team owns this relationship? Not you for all of them. Delegate.

**Column 9: Last Touchpoint**
Date of last meaningful interaction. If it's been 3+ weeks for a high-influence stakeholder, that's a red flag.
