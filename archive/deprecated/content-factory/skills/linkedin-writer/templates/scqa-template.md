# SCQA Post Template (McKinsey Framework)

Use for data-heavy or strategic insight posts. This structure naturally builds tension.

## Structure

**S - Situation (1 sentence):** 
The context everyone agrees on.
"GCC enterprises are spending $4.2B on digital transformation this year."

**C - Complication (1-2 sentences):**
The thing that makes the situation problematic.
"But 70% of DT programs fail to deliver measurable ROI. The money is flowing, the results aren't."

**Q - Question (implied or explicit):**
The question the reader now has in their mind.
"So what separates the 30% that actually work?"

**A - Answer (3-4 short paragraphs):**
Your insight, backed by experience.
"After leading DT across 15 hospitals and 8 countries, I've found three patterns..."

## Example Post Using SCQA

GCC enterprises are spending $4.2B on digital transformation this year.

But 70% of those programs will fail to deliver measurable ROI. The money is flowing. The results aren't.

After leading DT programs across 15 hospitals and 8 countries, I found three patterns that separate the 30% that work:

1. They appoint a single cross-functional owner before buying any technology
2. They measure adoption metrics, not deployment metrics
3. They kill failing workstreams at month 3, not month 12

The hardest one? Number 3. Nobody wants to kill their own initiative.

What's your cut-off point for a program that isn't delivering?
