# GPT Image 2.0 - Ahmed Brand Visual System

## Core rule
Use GPT Image 2.0 for:
- hero visuals
- portraits
- mood
- lighting
- realism
- composition

Do not use it for:
- final text-heavy cards
- exact template recreation
- typography layout
- infographic precision

## Approved visual lanes

### Lane A - Executive Editorial
Use for:
- authority posts
- leadership POV
- profile-grade visuals

Look:
- tailored dark suit
- premium office or city backdrop
- cinematic side lighting
- calm confidence
- magazine-level realism

### Lane B - Operator in Motion
Use for:
- scale
- execution
- PMO
- transformation stories

Look:
- Ahmed moving through strategy or operations environment
- glass, screens, motion, structure
- active but composed
- execution energy

### Lane C - AI Strategy
Use for:
- AI thought leadership
- automation
- decision velocity
- systems posts

Look:
- elegant modern workspace
- subtle advanced-tech cues
- believable screens and environment
- no sci-fi nonsense

### Lane D - Statement Visual
Use for:
- bold quote posts
- text overlay posts
- campaign visuals

Look:
- one strong portrait
- clean negative space
- restrained background
- manual typography added later

## Workflow

### Step 1 - Brief
Lock:
- post purpose
- chosen lane
- mood
- crop and composition
- whether text overlay will be added later

### Step 2 - Generate concepts
Create 3 concepts only:
- safe
- cinematic
- bold

### Step 3 - Pick one lane
Do not keep mixing concepts.

### Step 4 - Refine surgically
Only improve:
- likeness
- posture
- wardrobe
- lighting
- realism
- negative space

### Step 5 - Finish outside GPT Image
Add manually:
- headline
- footer
- divider
- CTA
- branding

## Master prompts

### Prompt A - Executive Authority
Create a premium editorial portrait of Ahmed Nasr, a senior executive in AI-enabled execution and transformation leadership. He is wearing a tailored dark suit with a crisp white shirt in a modern executive environment. Cinematic soft side lighting, realistic skin texture, calm confident expression, shallow depth of field, premium magazine photography style, believable background, subtle luxury, clean composition. Leave negative space for later text overlay. Avoid fake text, plastic skin, clutter, exaggerated futurism, distorted hands, and influencer-style posing.

### Prompt B - Scale and Operations
Create a cinematic leadership visual of Ahmed Nasr in a modern operations or command-center environment, wearing an elegant business suit, composed under pressure, moving through a structured high-performance workspace with glass, screens, and subtle operational activity in the background. Premium editorial realism, intelligent lighting, strong composition, high-trust business aesthetic, realistic face and posture, negative space for future typography. Avoid clutter, fake dashboards, sci-fi glow, and dramatic action-movie exaggeration.

### Prompt C - AI Strategy
Create a premium visual of Ahmed Nasr as an executive leader shaping AI for execution, visibility, and decision velocity. He is in an elegant corporate environment with subtle advanced technology cues, refined blue and graphite palette, realistic portrait, sophisticated lighting, strong composition, believable premium business setting, authoritative and modern tone. Keep the image realistic and restrained. Avoid hologram clichés, fake UI text, flashy neon effects, and over-designed futurism.

### Prompt D - Statement Poster Base
Create a premium portrait-based campaign visual of Ahmed Nasr with strong composition and clean negative space for a headline overlay. Tailored dark suit, refined posture, modern editorial lighting, realistic face, luxury business-magazine look, minimal background, high contrast but elegant color grading, visually powerful and simple. Make it feel authoritative, strategic, and premium. Avoid any text inside the image, cluttered scenes, fake logos, distorted hands, and cheap motivational-poster energy.

## Negative prompt block
Avoid:
- fake text
- extra fingers
- distorted hands
- plastic skin
- cartoon look
- cheesy grin
- startup-bro vibe
- sci-fi holograms
- cluttered dashboards
- low-end corporate stock photo feel
- over-saturated glow
- warped suit details
- unrealistic eyes
- bad teeth
- messy background objects

## Refinement prompt block
Refine this image while keeping the same concept and composition. Improve facial likeness to Ahmed Nasr, make the posture more natural and executive, improve realism in the suit and hands, clean the background, strengthen premium lighting, preserve negative space, and make the result feel more editorial, credible, and high-trust.

## QA checklist
Before approval, check:
- Does this actually look like Ahmed?
- Does it feel executive, not influencer?
- Are hands clean?
- Is the suit believable?
- Is the lighting premium?
- Is there enough negative space?
- Will text fit without crowding?
- Does it still look strong on mobile?
- Is the environment believable?
- Is the tone strategic, calm, and premium?

## Operating rule for LinkedIn visuals
For every post:
- GPT Image makes the base visual
- we manually add the text system
- one approved lane per post
- no random retries after lane selection
- judge on mobile impact, not desktop only

## Default lane selection
- Lane A for authority
- Lane B for execution and scaling
- Lane C for AI strategy
- Lane D for statement-card posts
