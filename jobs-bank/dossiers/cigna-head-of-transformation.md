## Job Description

The Head of Transformation is accountable for end-to-end transformation delivery across Cigna Healthcare Middle East & Africa, spanning operating model redesign, technology and data modernization, regulatory readiness, and execution discipline. This role operates in a highly regulated, fast-scaling environment, market buildouts, complex government integrations.

Reports to the MEA CEO and serves as a core member of the MEA leadership team.

### Key Responsibilities

**Transformation Leadership & Operating Model**
- Define, own, and continuously re-prioritize the MEA transformation roadmap
- Authority to recommend stopping, pausing, or re-scoping initiatives
- Evolve and lead the Transformation Office (TO) including Lean Portfolio Management
- Institutionalize agile and product-led operating models across MEA
- Partner with business and support functions to align operating model changes, budget cycles, workforce plans, vendor strategies

**Program & Initiative Delivery**
- Own delivery of multi-year enterprise transformation portfolio: digital distribution, customer self-service, claims and medical cost transformation, provider network enablement, core platform modernization
- Accountable for delivery outcomes across matrixed environment (Operations, Technology, Finance, Markets, third-party vendors)
- Drive cross-functional collaboration (Scrum of Scrums, ART Sync)
- Institutionalize benefits realization tracking

**Change Management & Culture**
- Champion agile and customer-centric mindsets
- Coach executives and teams on lean/agile, product ownership, hypothesis-driven delivery
- Design and execute change plans using proven frameworks
- Build transformation capability (RTEs, Scrum Masters, Product Owners, PMs, BAs)

**Data, Technology & Process Modernization**
- Advance data and analytics foundations (data quality, governance, BI/self-service, AI/ML use cases)
- Lead process re-engineering and automation (Lean, Six Sigma, workflow digitization, RPA)
- Partner with Technology for platform modernization, data foundations, integration simplification

**Benefits Realization & Value Tracking**
- Own benefits realization end-to-end with linkage between initiatives and financial, operational, regulatory outcomes
