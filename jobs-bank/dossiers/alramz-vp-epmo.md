## Job Description

VP EPMO to establish and lead centralized enterprise function responsible for strategic oversight and governance across Al Ramz Group.

### Key Responsibilities

**Enterprise Planning, Demand Management & Standardization**
- Lead enterprise demand forecasting, consolidate project requests across BUs
- Establish prioritization frameworks aligned with strategy
- Develop standardized methodologies, governance models, tools, PM practices

**Strategic Execution & Business Planning**
- Translate corporate strategy into executable initiative roadmaps
- Support annual and multi-year business plans with delivery feasibility, resource modelling

**Portfolio Governance & Delivery Oversight**
- Maintain enterprise visibility across all programs and projects
- Track progress, dependencies, budgets, risks, benefits realization
- Conduct periodic portfolio reviews and audits
- Provide executive dashboards and decision-support analytics

**Strategic Alignment & Organizational Enablement**
- Ensure initiatives align with corporate objectives
- Advise leadership on investment prioritization and resource allocation
- Mentor department heads on governance, planning, execution

**Performance Monitoring & Value Realization**
- Track enterprise portfolios against KPIs
- Ensure benefits realization frameworks applied to all major initiatives

**Risk, Compliance & Governance Assurance**
- Identify enterprise initiative risks, develop mitigation strategies
- Ensure compliance with regulatory, internal policy, audit requirements

### Qualifications
- Bachelor's in IT, CS, Business Admin or Finance
- 10+ years experience, 5+ in financial services or management consulting
- PMP or PGMP required (desirable: MBA)
- Financial and stock market trading business knowledge
- IT background
