## Job Description

VP of Digital Strategy & Product to lead digital transformation and product evolution of Abu Dhabi Food Hub (ADFH) - the region's first AI-powered food trade and distribution ecosystem. Operating as part of AD Ports Group.

### Key Responsibilities

**Strategic Leadership**
- Lead digital transformation strategy and roadmap for ADFH
- Present strategic recommendations to board level stakeholders
- Make critical partnership decisions evaluating global platforms vs proprietary development
- Own business case and ROI modeling for digital marketplace and AI capabilities

**Product & Platform Development**
- Define digital marketplace product strategy (B2B trade, tenant services, value-added services)
- Drive AI-powered capabilities (demand forecasting, predictive analytics, intelligent supply chain optimization)
- Establish data architecture foundation
- Manage integration with Abu Dhabi Trade & Logistics Platform (ATLP) and government systems

**Technology Implementation (Launch Phase)**
- Oversee Phase I technology systems (CRM, ERP, access management) with July 2026 go-live
- Lead RFP processes and vendor selection
- Ensure coordination between technology and physical facility readiness for October 2026 launch

**Stakeholder & Matrix Management**
- Collaborate with KEZAD CTO and technology team
- Work cross-functionally with Commercial, Operations, Administration
- Influence without direct authority in matrix environment

**Three-Horizon Roadmap:**
- Horizon 1: Smart physical operations
- Horizon 2: Digital marketplace platform
- Horizon 3: Regional AI-powered platform
