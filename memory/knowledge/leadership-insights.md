## Eric Schmidt: The Great Founders Are Frugal | 2026-02-26

**Source:** Email from Startup Archive (mike@startuparchive.org)
**Type:** Article / Quote

**Key Insights:**
- Frugality is a distinguishing trait of successful founders
- Schmidt joined Google in 2001, shared an office with four engineers, lent a founder his car
- Warning sign: beautiful offices and nice chairs = "I cringe"
- Best founders often take zero salary initially
- Money should be used precisely for certain areas, not lifestyle perks
