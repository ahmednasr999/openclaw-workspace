### Fix 2: ATS Gate on Career Verdict (DONE)
- **Root cause:** Two different scores: LLM career fit (1-10, title-based) vs ATS keyword score (0-100, JD-based). Verdict used LLM score only, ignoring weak JD match.
- **Example:** Capco "Director Data" got 9/10 career fit but ATS 47 - SUBMIT verdict despite weak JD match.
- **Fix:** If `verdict == "APPLY"` AND `20 <= ats < 55`, cap verdict at `"STRETCH"` with fit capped at 6.
- **ATS < 20 = "couldn't fetch JD"** - not penalized (15 out of 28 SUBMIT jobs had ATS < 20)
- **ATS 20-54 = "JD was read, weak match"** - gated to STRETCH (11 out of 28)
- **ATS >= 55 = clean pass** (2 out of 28: Combuzz 63, Capco 60)

### Score Mismatch Explanation
- `ats_score` in `jobs-summary.json` (from scanner/review) ≠ `ATS Score` in Notion (from pipeline_db → notion_client)
- Two separate scoring moments with different data availability
- Not a bug to fix - just a known divergence between scanner score and deep JD score
