## The 10 Use Cases

### 1. Buying Intent Sniping
- Monitors X, Reddit, Quora for posts like "need alternative to X"
- Replies when intent is real
- Result: ~50 visits per 1,000 views
- Warning: X bans at 200+ automated replies/day

### 2. AI Slop Machine
- Posts content across 20+ platforms in each platform's style
- LinkedIn, Medium, Substack, Reddit, Dev.to, etc.
- Cost: ~$1/topic, 100 topics/mo = 2,000 pieces for $100
- Result: 200+ visits/day within a week

### 3. Directory Submissions
- Submits to 100+ directories (Product Hunt, BetaList, etc.)
- Gradual submission over weeks
- Helps SEO and citations

### 4. TikTok Content Factory
- Oliver Henry's approach (covered separately)
- 60 seconds human work per post

### 5. Autonomous X Reply Guy
- Scans feed 24/7, replies to relevant conversations
- Result: 400 followers in 4 days, ~7 demos

### 6. Job Posting Sniper
- Monitors job boards for "Hiring: X"
- Finds hiring manager's email
- Sends pitch: "Try AI agent at $500/mo instead of hiring"

### 7. (Skipped)

### 8. SEO Keyword Gap Exploitation
- Compares sitemap vs competitors
- Finds keywords they rank for that you don't
- Generates content per gap

### 9. Telegram/Discord Infiltration
- Joins 20-30 relevant channels
- Replies to questions your product solves

### 10. Self-Improving Skill Files
- Every failure = new rule
- Every success = new formula
- Agent compounds over time
