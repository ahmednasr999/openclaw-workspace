# LinkedIn & AI Visibility Strategy

*Source: Ashpreet Bedi*
*Saved: 2026-02-21*

---

## Key Data Points

- **5,000+ prompts** analyzed in software purchase space
- **1.2 million citations** examined

---

## Surprising Findings

| Finding | Implication |
|---------|-------------|
| **LinkedIn > Slashdot/SourceForge** | LinkedIn has more LLM influence than traditional dev platforms |
| **Company pages don't matter** | Personal content > Company pages |
| **3 likes can perform well** | Small engagement = big AI visibility |
| **75% of citations = LinkedIn Pulse** | Long-form posts dominate |
| **AI Advice pages get cited** | AI-generated content still valuable |
| **AI Mode loves LinkedIn > AI Overview** | LinkedIn > Google for AI answers |

---

## Action Items for Your Job Search

1. **Post on LinkedIn Pulse** - Long-form content gets cited by LLMs
2. **Quality > Quantity** - Even 3 likes is enough
3. **Personal > Company** - Your personal brand matters
4. **AI Advice content** - Write about career advice, industry insights
5. **Focus on LinkedIn** - More valuable than other platforms for AI visibility
