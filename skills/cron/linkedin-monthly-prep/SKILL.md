# LinkedIn Monthly Content Prep

Monthly planning session for next month's LinkedIn content strategy.

## Prerequisites
- Last month's Content Calendar data
- LinkedIn Analytics data
- Content Intelligence insights

## Steps

### Step 1: Review last month's performance
- Total posts published
- Average engagement rate
- Best performing post (topic + format)
- Worst performing post (why it didn't work)

### Step 2: Set monthly theme
Based on:
- Job search priorities (what roles are you targeting?)
- Industry trends (what's hot in AI/PMO/transformation?)
- Seasonal relevance (conferences, events, hiring cycles)

### Step 3: Plan weekly themes
Week 1-4 themes that build on each other. Example:
- Week 1: Thought leadership on AI
- Week 2: Case study from experience
- Week 3: GCC market insight
- Week 4: Career/leadership reflection

### Step 4: Generate content calendar
Create 12-16 post slots in Notion Content Calendar:
- 3-4 posts per week
- Mix of formats (story, listicle, hot take, question)
- Status: "💡 Ideas"

### Step 5: Report
"🗓️ Monthly content plan ready: [X] posts planned across [themes]. First pre-build Friday."

## Error Handling
- If analytics unavailable: Plan based on qualitative assessment
- If Notion unreachable: Save plan to `content-monthly-plan.md`

## Output Rules
- No em dashes. Hyphens only.
- Themes must be specific, not vague
