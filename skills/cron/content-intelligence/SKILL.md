# LinkedIn Content Intelligence - Thursday Pre-Build Feed

Research what's working on LinkedIn this week to inform content strategy.

## Prerequisites
- Web search access (Brave/Tavily)
- Notion Content Calendar DB: `3268d599-a162-814b-8854-c9b8bde62468`
- Knowledge of Ahmed's domains: AI automation, digital transformation, PMO excellence, GCC market

## Steps

### Step 1: Research trending topics
Search for trending LinkedIn content in these domains:
- AI in enterprise / automation
- Digital transformation in healthcare and GCC
- PMO and program management leadership
- Saudi Vision 2030, UAE digital economy

### Step 2: Analyze top performers
For each domain, find 3-5 posts that got high engagement. Note:
- Topic and angle
- Format (story, listicle, hot take, case study)
- Hook style (question, bold claim, statistic)
- Engagement metrics if visible

### Step 3: Generate content angles
Create 5-7 content angles Ahmed could use, each with:
- Topic
- Suggested hook (first line)
- Format recommendation
- Why it would work (based on trending data)
- Relevance to Ahmed's positioning

### Step 4: Update Content Calendar
Add suggested topics to Notion Content Calendar with status "💡 Ideas".
Include the research backing for each suggestion.

### Step 5: Report
Send Telegram summary: "📊 Content Intel: [X] trending topics analyzed, [Y] angles generated for next week"

## Error Handling
- If search fails: Use cached knowledge of evergreen topics
- If Notion unreachable: Save angles locally to `content-intel-backup.md`

## Quality Gates
- Minimum 5 unique content angles
- Each angle must connect to Ahmed's expertise
- No duplicate topics from last 2 weeks
- Must include at least 1 GCC-specific angle

## Output Rules
- No em dashes. Hyphens only.
- Angles must be specific, not generic ("AI is changing business" = bad)
