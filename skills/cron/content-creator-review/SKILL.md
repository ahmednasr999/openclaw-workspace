# Content Creator - Weekly Domain Review

Friday review of Ahmed's LinkedIn content domain. Assess what worked, what didn't, and plan improvements.

## Prerequisites
- Notion Content Calendar DB
- Notion LinkedIn Analytics DB: `3268d599-a162-811a-b028-e06c181bfd85`
- Last week's posts and engagement data

## Steps

### Step 1: Pull this week's content data
- Posts published this week (from Content Calendar)
- Engagement metrics per post (likes, comments, impressions)
- Compare to previous week's performance

### Step 2: Identify patterns
- Which topics got the most engagement?
- Which formats worked best?
- Best posting times?
- Audience segments that engaged most?

### Step 3: Strategy alignment check
- Are posts supporting the job search narrative?
- Is the AI/PMO/transformation positioning consistent?
- Any missed opportunities this week?

### Step 4: Generate recommendations
- Top 3 things to keep doing
- Top 3 things to change
- 1 experiment to try next week

### Step 5: Update Notion
- Add review notes to Content Calendar
- Update LinkedIn Analytics with weekly summary

### Step 6: Report
Send Telegram summary with key takeaways and next week's focus.

## Error Handling
- If no posts this week: Review last 2 weeks instead, flag content gap
- If analytics unavailable: Use qualitative assessment from post interactions

## Output Rules
- No em dashes. Hyphens only.
- Be specific with numbers, not "good engagement"
