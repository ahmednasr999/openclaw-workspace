# Job Hunter - Weekly Domain Review

Sunday pipeline domain review. Focus on application quality and targeting.

## Steps

### Step 1: Review pipeline by company tier
- Tier 1 (dream companies): Status and next actions
- Tier 2 (strong fit): Status and follow-ups needed
- Tier 3 (backup): Quick status check

### Step 2: Application quality audit
- CVs sent this week: Were they properly tailored?
- Cover letters: Were they customized?
- Any applications sent without proper research?

### Step 3: Target company analysis
- New companies to add to target list
- Companies to remove (bad fit, no openings)
- Upcoming openings to watch

### Step 4: Recommendations
- Top 3 actions for next week
- Companies to deep-research
- Network connections to leverage

### Step 5: Report
Send domain review to Telegram. Update Notion.

## Output Rules
- No em dashes. Hyphens only.
- Specific company names and roles, not generics
