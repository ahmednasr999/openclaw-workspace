# Ahmed's Professional Communication Voice — HR Context

## Tone
Direct, confident, peer-to-peer. Ahmed speaks as a senior executive to other senior professionals - not as a job seeker asking for favors.

## Outreach Principles
- Lead with value, not need ("I bring 20 years of..." not "I'm looking for...")
- One clear ask per message
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- No desperation signals, no over-explaining
- Professional warmth without being casual

## Never Say
❌ "I would be honored to..." (submissive)
❌ "I hope this finds you well" (generic filler)
❌ "Please find attached my CV" (passive)
❌ "I'm currently unemployed/between roles" (negative framing)
❌ "Any opportunity would be great" (unfocused)
❌ Exclamation marks in professional outreach

## Always Include
✅ Specific reason for contacting THIS person (not a mass blast)
✅ One quantified achievement relevant to the target (e.g., "$50M transformation")
✅ Clear next step (call, meeting, intro request)
✅ Ahmed's LinkedIn URL

## Email Style
- Subject lines: specific and value-led, not generic
- Body: 4-6 sentences maximum
- Sign-off: "Ahmed Nasr" (no titles in signature for cold outreach)

## Follow-Up Style
- Shorter than original message
- Reference the previous message briefly
- Add one new piece of value or context
- Never guilt-trip ("I sent you a message last week...")

## LinkedIn Message Style (≤300 chars for InMail preview)
- First line must hook (who Ahmed is + why reaching out)
- Second line: the ask
- No life story

## Cover Letter Voice
- More formal than LinkedIn, less formal than a legal brief
- First paragraph: why this company specifically (not generic)
- Middle: 2-3 achievement bullets mirroring JD keywords
- Close: confident availability statement + next step
- 1 page maximum, always
