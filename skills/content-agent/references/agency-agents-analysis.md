## What We Took

### From LinkedIn Content Creator (marketing/marketing-linkedin-content-creator.md)
- Post type taxonomy: Story/Expertise/Opinion/Data (we added BTS as 5th)
- Hook engineering: 3 variants per post (curiosity gap, bold claim, specific story)
- Content mix ratios with tracking
- "No links in body" rule
- Comment-to-pipeline system (5 comments before posting)
- Voice profile with on-voice/off-voice examples
- Profile as landing page concept
- AI citation: structure posts for ChatGPT/Claude citability

### From Carousel Growth Engine (marketing/marketing-carousel-growth-engine.md)
- `learnings.json` pattern → our `content-performance.json`
- "Phase 1 is Learn" → read performance data BEFORE creating
- Self-improving loop: correlate post attributes with engagement
- Vision-based QA → our quality gate checklist

### From NEXUS Strategy (strategy/)
- Quality gates between phases with evidence
- Dev↔QA loop → draft → check → revise (max 2) → escalate
- Structured handoffs with full context

### From Growth Hacker (marketing/marketing-growth-hacker.md)
- Experiment velocity: systematic A/B testing one variable at a time
- Monthly experiment tracker with statistical rigor
- "30% winner rate" expectation (most experiments won't win, that's fine)

### From Content Creator (marketing/marketing-content-creator.md)
- Content repurposing system: top performers get reformatted
- Content cascade: primary format → adapted versions

### From TikTok + Instagram (convergence insight)
- Engagement velocity: first-hour window is universal across platforms
- "Golden Hour" protocol with alerts
- Pre-post priming (comment on others first)

### From Reddit Community Builder (marketing/marketing-reddit-community-builder.md)
- 90/10 rule applied to LinkedIn comments: 100% value, zero self-promotion
- Value-first engagement as relationship building

### From Visual Storyteller (design/design-visual-storyteller.md)
- Narrative arc in visual sequence
- Image genres matched to content types
- Information design principles for data posts

### From Brand Guardian (design/design-brand-guardian.md)
- Brand compliance as a checkable system
- Consistency scoring concept

### From AI Citation Strategist (marketing/marketing-ai-citation-strategist.md)
- AEO ≠ SEO (different signals for AI engines)
- Named concept/framework pattern for citability
- Entity clarity + structured authority = AI citations
- Queued for Q2 deeper implementation
