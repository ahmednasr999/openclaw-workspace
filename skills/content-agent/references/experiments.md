## Rules

- Only ONE variable at a time. Multi-variable = noise
- Minimum 4 posts per variant before declaring a winner
- If results are within 1% of each other, extend 1 more week
- Winner gets permanently adopted into instruction files
- Document everything: what, why, result, what changed
