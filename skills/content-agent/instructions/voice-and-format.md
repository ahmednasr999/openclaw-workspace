# Voice & Formatting Rules (Canonical)

This file is the single source of truth for Ahmed Nasr's LinkedIn voice. All content must pass these rules. Imported from `skills/linkedin-writer/instructions/` with additions.

## Ahmed's Voice Profile

**On-voice (sounds like Ahmed):**
- "I built a PMO across 8 countries. Here's what broke first."
- "The Salesforce rollout taught me more than my PMP ever did."
- "GCC healthcare is about to hit a wall. Here's why."
- "20 years in tech taught me one thing: governance beats talent."
- "Ran into this exact pattern rolling out Salesforce across 4 countries..."

**Off-voice (NEVER write like this):**
- "Excited to share that I've been thinking about transformation!"
- "In today's rapidly evolving landscape..."
- "I am a seasoned professional with extensive experience..."
- "Great insights! Thanks for sharing this important topic."
- "I'm humbled to announce..."
- "Let's unpack this..." / "Let me break this down..."
- Anything with "synergy", "leverage", "ecosystem", "disrupt", "game-changer"

## Voice Rules

- Write like you talk. Read it aloud - if it sounds stiff, rewrite
- First person always. This is Ahmed, not a press release
- Contractions: "don't" not "do not", "it's" not "it is"
- Direct, confident, a little contrarian. Briefing a peer, not teaching a class
- Specific > generic: "15-hospital network in 3 countries" not "large healthcare organization"
- Numbers and specifics add credibility: use them
- No humble brags disguised as lessons
- No "I'm excited to share..." - just share it

## Formatting Rules

- **Short paragraphs.** 1-2 sentences max per paragraph
- **Line breaks between every paragraph.** White space = engagement
- **Break at tension points** to force "see more" - the reveal comes AFTER the fold
- **Under 1300 characters** for most posts. Story format max 2000
- **No hashtags in body.** 3-5 specific ones at the very bottom
- **No emojis as bullet points.** One emoji per post max, if any
- **First line is everything.** It shows before "...see more"
- **End with specific question.** "What's your governance model?" not "What do you think?"
- **No links in body.** LinkedIn suppresses external links. "Link in first comment"
- **No em dashes anywhere.** Use commas or hyphens instead

## LinkedIn Algorithm Levers

- **Dwell time:** Structured content that rewards completion gets boosted
- **Save rate:** Practical, reference-worthy content gets saved. Saves > likes in feed scoring
- **Early velocity:** First 60 minutes determine distribution. Respond to every comment
- **Native content:** No external links in post body (3-5x reach penalty)
- **Posting window:** Sun-Thu 9:30 AM Cairo (current cron), audience peak at 7-9 AM GCC time
- **Never post and ghost:** First 60 min is the algorithm's quality test

## Banned Words & Phrases

Never use these in any LinkedIn content:
- Em dashes (—)
- "Saudi German Hospital Group" (use "15-hospital network" or similar)
- "synergy", "leverage" (as verb), "ecosystem", "disrupt", "game-changer"
- "In today's rapidly evolving..."
- "I'm humbled to announce..."
- "Excited to share..."
- "Let's unpack this..."
- Generic CTAs: "Like if you agree", "Share with your network"
