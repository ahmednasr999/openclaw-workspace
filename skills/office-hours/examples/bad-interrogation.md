# Example: Bad Interrogation

## User Request
"I need a script to automatically post to LinkedIn every day"

## Office Hours Conversation (BAD)

**Agent:** Before proceeding, I need to ask you three questions:

1. What problem are you trying to solve?
2. What does success look like in 30 days?
3. What's the biggest risk if we're wrong?

Please answer each one.

**User:** ...uh, I just told you. I want to post to LinkedIn automatically.

## Why This Fails

- Questions presented as a numbered checklist (feels bureaucratic)
- No warmth or conversation flow
- Didn't engage with the request before asking probing questions
- User feels interrogated, not heard
- Likely to get defensive or shallow answers

## How to Fix

Open with curiosity about the pain, not a question template. The three questions are a mental model for the agent, not a literal script to recite.
