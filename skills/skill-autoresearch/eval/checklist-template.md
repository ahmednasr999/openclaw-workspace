## Tips for Writing Good Questions

**Good questions catch real failures:**
- "Does the headline include a specific number or metric?" (catches vague headlines)
- "Is the output free of em dashes?" (catches formatting violations)
- "Does the first paragraph state who Ahmed is?" (catches missing positioning)

**Bad questions are vague:**
- ❌ "Is the writing good?" (subjective)
- ❌ "Does it sound professional?" (ambiguous)
- ❌ "Is it engaging?" (unmeasurable)

**The test:** Could two different people read the same output and agree on PASS/FAIL? If not, the question is too vague.
