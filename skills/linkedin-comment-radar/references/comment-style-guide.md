# LinkedIn Comment Style Guide (Enforced)

## Hard rules
1. Start with an insight, never with generic praise.
2. Include one concrete anchor: governance, cadence, owner, metric, or value realization.
3. Keep 2 to 4 lines max.
4. Add one sharp question in about 40 percent of comments.
5. No em dashes.
6. Ban these openers: Strong point, Great, Excellent, Important signal, Very accurate, Very relevant.
7. Prefer tension framing: strategy vs execution, speed vs control, innovation vs governance.

## Ahmed voice markers
- Executive, practical, decision-oriented.
- Focus on operating reality and business outcomes.
- Avoid hype and soft praise.
