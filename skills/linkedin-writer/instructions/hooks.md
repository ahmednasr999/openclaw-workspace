# Hook Engineering

The first line must stop the scroll and earn the "...see more" click. Nothing else matters if this fails.

## Generate 3 Hook Variants Per Post

**Type 1 - Curiosity Gap:**
- "I almost turned down the role that taught me more than my MBA."
- "There's a platform tracking the entire planet in real time."
- "The meeting that saved a $50M program lasted 7 minutes."

**Type 2 - Bold Claim:**
- "Most digital transformation roadmaps fail for the same reason most diets do."
- "Your PMO is probably measuring the wrong things."
- "The best CTO I ever worked with had no CS degree."

**Type 3 - Specific Story:**
- "Tuesday, 3 AM. I'm staring at a Salesforce migration about to blow up across 4 countries."
- "My dad never understood what I do. Last week, he finally did."
- "8 countries. 15 hospitals. One governance framework. Here's what happened."

**For job seekers:**
"I applied to 94 jobs. 3 responded. Here's what changed everything."

## Hook Rules
- Title: 8-15 words max, single sentence
- Never use emoji in titles
- Never list credentials in the opening
- Never start with context or setup - start with the bold thing
- Test: would YOUR target reader stop scrolling for this?
