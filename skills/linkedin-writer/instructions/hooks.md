# Hook Engineering

The first line must stop the scroll and earn the "...see more" click. Nothing else matters if this fails.

## The Hook Formula (Ruben Hassid, 770K followers)

A hook is exactly **2 lines of ~55 characters each** — the visible text before "...see more" on LinkedIn.

**Its only job:** Break the reader's scrolling pattern. Create an open loop they NEED to close.

## Generate 10 Hook Variants Per Post (by technique)

**Technique 1 - Contradiction** (say something that sounds wrong):
- "The worst LinkedIn posts get the most followers."
- "Your PMO is costing you more than it saves."
- "The executives who get hired fastest stop applying."

**Technique 2 - Specific Number + Unexpected Context:**
- "I applied to 94 jobs. 3 responded. Here's what changed."
- "8 countries. 15 hospitals. One broken framework."
- "I mass-unfollowed 2,000 people. My reach tripled."

**Technique 3 - Direct Accusation** (call the reader out):
- "You're writing LinkedIn posts for your mom, not your audience."
- "Your digital transformation roadmap is a deck, not a plan."
- "You're optimizing for the interview. Not the job."

**Technique 4 - Stolen Thought** (say what they secretly think but won't):
- "You know your LinkedIn posts are boring. So does everyone scrolling past."
- "Most PMOs exist to make leaders feel safe. Not to deliver value."
- "You've been told to 'network more.' You hate it. So do I."

**Technique 5 - Absurd Reframe** (take something mundane and make it dramatic):
- "Your LinkedIn hook has 1.2 seconds to live. Most die instantly."
- "A 7-minute meeting saved a $50M program. Here's exactly why."
- "Tuesday, 3 AM. A Salesforce migration about to collapse 4 countries."

## Hook Rules
- Exactly 2 short lines, ~55 characters each
- Must be about THE READER or a universal tension — never about Ahmed's achievement
- Creates an open loop: unanswered question, contradiction, or bold claim
- No emoji openers, no hashtags in the hook
- No personal achievements as openers ("I'm proud to announce...")
- Test: would a stranger stop scrolling and think "wait, what?"

## Delivery Format
Always present all 10 hooks organized by technique. Let Ahmed pick. Never pick for him — taste is the competitive advantage.
