# Hook Engineering

The first line must stop the scroll and earn the "...see more" click. Nothing else matters if this fails.

## The Hook Formula (Ruben Hassid, 770K followers)

A hook is exactly **2 lines of ~55 characters each** — the visible text before "...see more" on LinkedIn.

**Its only job:** Break the reader's scrolling pattern. Create an open loop they NEED to close.

## Generate 10 Hook Variants Per Post (by technique)

These are Ahmed-specific templates derived from Leon Abboud's 9 authority hooks + Ruben Hassid's 5 techniques. Use both banks.

---

### BANK 1: Ruben Hassid's 5 Scroll-Stop Techniques (Format + Surprise)

**Technique 1 - Contradiction** (say something that sounds wrong):
- "The worst LinkedIn posts get the most followers."
- "Your PMO is costing you more than it saves."
- "The executives who get hired fastest stop applying."
- "The best program managers I know hate project management tools."

**Technique 2 - Specific Number + Unexpected Context:**
- "I applied to 94 jobs. 3 responded. Here's what changed."
- "8 countries. 15 hospitals. One broken framework."
- "20 years in enterprise tech. The last 6 months made all of it obsolete."
- "I gave an AI agent my entire job description. It finished in 40 minutes."

**Technique 3 - Direct Accusation** (call the reader out):
- "You're writing LinkedIn posts for your mom, not your audience."
- "Your digital transformation roadmap is a deck, not a plan."
- "You're optimizing for the interview. Not the job."
- "Your PMO reports are beautiful. Your projects still fail on time."

**Technique 4 - Stolen Thought** (say what they secretly think but won't):
- "You know your LinkedIn posts are boring. So does everyone scrolling past."
- "Most PMOs exist to make leaders feel safe. Not to deliver value."
- "You've been told to 'network more.' You hate it. So do I."
- "Every exec knows their digital transformation is 60% theater. Nobody says it."

**Technique 5 - Absurd Reframe** (take something mundane and make it dramatic):
- "Your LinkedIn hook has 1.2 seconds to live. Most die instantly."
- "A 7-minute meeting saved a $50M program. Here's exactly why."
- "Tuesday, 3 AM. A Salesforce migration about to collapse 4 countries."
- "I ran a $200M healthcare program. My most important tool cost $20/month."

### BANK 2: Leon Abboud's 9 Authority/Expertise Hooks (Ahmed-Adapted)

These are for **expertise posts** — make Ahmed's 20+ years of specific experience visible.

1. **The Pattern Hook:**
   "I've led digital transformation across [X] hospitals in [Y] countries. They all make the same mistake: [specific mistake]."
   *Template: "I've [credential]. They all [surprising pattern]."*

2. **The Contrarian Proof Hook:**
   "Everyone says enterprise transformation needs 3-5 years. I've seen the opposite work dozens of times. Here's what actually happens:"
   *Template: "Everyone says [common advice]. I've seen the opposite work [X] times."*

3. **The Turning Point Hook:**
   "[X] years ago I was managing a 15-hospital EHR rollout with no AI, no automation, 6 coordinators, and a failing timeline. Today the same scope runs with 2 people and no misses. The turning point was [one thing]."
   *Template: "[X] years ago I [struggle]. Today I [result]. The turning point was [one thing]."*

4. **The Transformation Witness Hook:**
   "I watched a program director go from 3 failed deployments to flawless execution in 90 days. They changed one thing: [thing]."
   *Template: "I watched [person type] go from [bad] to [good] in [timeframe]. One thing changed."*

5. **The Client Result Hook:**
   "A client was spending $400K/year on a PMO reporting function that nobody read. We replaced it with one AI agent. Within 60 days, [specific result]."
   *Template: "A client was spending $[X] on [thing] and getting nothing. We [changed one thing]. [Specific result]."*

6. **The Invisible Pattern Hook:**
   "There's a pattern I keep seeing in healthcare IT programs that nobody talks about: [pattern]. Here's why it costs $10M when it hits:"
   *Template: "There's a pattern in [industry] nobody talks about: [pattern]."*

7. **The Expensive Lesson Hook:**
   "I made a $2M budget mistake in a government health program. I'm sharing it publicly so you don't repeat it."
   *Template: "I spent $[X] learning this the hard way so you don't have to."*

8. **The Data Pattern Hook:**
   "[X] enterprise transformation programs. [Y]% had the same root cause for delay. Here's what separates the ones that delivered from the ones that didn't:"
   *Template: "[Specific number] of [things analyzed]. [%] had [surprising finding]."*

9. **The Stop Doing Hook:**
   "Stop building PMO dashboards for your steering committee. It's killing your credibility with your delivery team. Here's what to build instead — based on 20 years of both sides:"
   *Template: "Stop [common thing]. It's costing you [consequence]. Here's what to do instead."*

## Hook Rules
- Exactly 2 short lines, ~55 characters each
- Must be about THE READER or a universal tension — never about Ahmed's achievement
- Creates an open loop: unanswered question, contradiction, or bold claim
- No emoji openers, no hashtags in the hook
- No personal achievements as openers ("I'm proud to announce...")
- Test: would a stranger stop scrolling and think "wait, what?"

## Delivery Format
Always present all 10 hooks organized by technique. Let Ahmed pick. Never pick for him — taste is the competitive advantage.
