# Quality Check Checklist

- [ ] Hook would make you stop scrolling (test: would YOU click "see more"?)
- [ ] Sounds like Ahmed, not a brand or content mill
- [ ] Has white space (short paragraphs with line breaks)
- [ ] Contains at least one specific detail (numbers, names, dates, countries)
- [ ] Ends with a specific engagement question (not "what do you think?")
- [ ] No cringe buzzwords (synergy, leverage, ecosystem, disrupt)
- [ ] Under 1300 characters (unless story format)
- [ ] No links in post body (use first comment)
- [ ] No em dashes anywhere
- [ ] Anchored in a content pillar (PMO, DT, AI, GCC, Leadership)
- [ ] Has a defensible point of view (not neutral/generic)
- [ ] 3-5 specific hashtags at bottom (not generic ones)
