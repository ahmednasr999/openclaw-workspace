### 1. Positioning Gap
- **Problem:** "Digital Transformation Executive" is too generic
- **Fix:** Specific positioning: "HealthTech AI Leader" or "Chief Digital Officer"
- **Impact:** Higher perceived value, fewer competitors

### 2. Visibility Problem  
- **Problem:** 20K LinkedIn followers dormant
- **Fix:** Weekly thought leadership content
- **Impact:** Recruiters reach out, not vice versa

### 3. Titling Error
- **Problem:** "PMO & Regional Engagement Lead" = Mid-level
- **Fix:** Target "Chief" or "VP" titles
- **Impact:** 50-100% salary increase

### 4. Industry Niche Trap
- **Problem:** HealthTech = smaller, competitive market
- **Fix:** Broaden to "Enterprise Digital Transformation" 
- **Impact:** 3x more opportunities

### 5. Geography Limit
- **Problem:** UAE-only = capped salaries
- **Fix:** Remote US/European executive roles
- **Impact:** 2-3x salary potential

### 6. Consulting Undervaluation
- **Problem:** ANCO exists but not positioned
- **Fix:** Position as fractional executive, charge premium
- **Impact:** $3,000-5,000/day vs $1,500

### 7. Self-Promotion Deficit
- **Problem:** $50M transformation buried in resume
- **Fix:** Front-load quantifiable achievements
- **Impact:** Recruiters see ROI immediately
